Ludo Rules Guide

🎮 Objective

Move all four of your colored tokens out of base, along the track, and safely into the center home triangle before your opponents do.

🎲 Roll and Start

Roll a 6 to release a token out of the starting base. Getting a 6 also awards an extra turn. Rolling a 6 three times in a row passes the turn.

🛡️ Safe Zones & Stars

Stars printed on the grid cells indicate standard safe areas. Tokens sitting on starred cells are protected and cannot be captured by other colors.

⚔️ Capturing Tokens

If you land directly on an opponent's token occupying a standard non-safe cell, you capture it. The captured token resets back to its home base, and you get an extra roll!

🏁 Reaching Home

After navigating clockwise around the track, your tokens step up the colored path toward the goal pocket. You must roll the exact number required to land in the center.
